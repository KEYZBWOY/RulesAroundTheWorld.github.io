Rules

USA

Speed limit on highways is typically 65-75 mph.

Drinking alcohol under the age of 21 is prohibited.

Carry a valid photo ID at all times.

UK

Drive on the left side of the road.

Speed limits vary depending on the road type and location.

Smoking is prohibited in enclosed public spaces.

Canada

Speed limits vary by province, typically 100 km/h on highways.

Drinking alcohol under the age of 19 (in most provinces) is prohibited.

Always carry government-issued photo identification.
